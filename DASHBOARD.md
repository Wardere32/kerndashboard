# DASHBOARD for the Kern CCD VR EEO Faculty Development and Peer Mentoring Program

## 1. Program Overview
The Kern CCD VR EEO Faculty Development and Peer Mentoring Program aims to enhance faculty capabilities in virtual reality (VR) through targeted professional development. This initiative focuses on creating an inclusive learning environment that fosters peer mentoring and utilizes VR technology to improve educational outcomes.

## 2. Key Activities Organized by Fiscal Year
### Fiscal Year 2024
- Conduct 3 training sessions on VR technology for faculty.
- Initiate peer mentoring groups paired across departments.

### Fiscal Year 2025
- Host additional 4 training sessions focusing on curriculum integration of VR.
- Evaluate peer mentoring effectiveness and make necessary adjustments.

### Fiscal Year 2026
- Expand peer mentoring structure based on feedback.
- Integrate new VR tools and equipment into the faculty training sessions.

## 3. Workplan
### Project Goals
- Train 100 faculty members in VR applications for education.
- Establish a sustainable peer mentoring program.

### Measurable Outcomes
- 90% of trained faculty report increased confidence in using VR tools.
- 75% of participants actively engaged in peer mentoring sessions.
